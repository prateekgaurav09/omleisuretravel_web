Our Company

We feel immense pleasure to introduce ourselves as a leading "Tourism House in India". Though we have moved out of the incubation stage since last five years and are in the process of getting a strong foothold in today arena.....yet we proudly declare our presence in a position where others dare to dream of.

During all these days we have rendered relentless service to many organisations, corporate houses, travel partners and individual clients. Our thrust area of activity being arrangement of conference, seminars, workshops, dealers associate meet, incentive tours to domestic destinations and abroad, pilgrimage tours , honeymoon packages, study tours and many others. We derive maximum satisfaction from one thing that during the course of our journey we have never lost a single customer but due to their referral there is remarkable growth of volume of business.

Being a result oriented organization we synergies our expertise and experience with a style of business that is in tune with the time OM LEISURE HOLIDAYS is a unique blend of smooth prompt and personalized service provider devoted to customer concern and satisfaction. We have established our branches at Delhi, Hyderabad, Bhubaneswar, Puri, Cuttack and Kolkata. With a work force of three hundred dedicated team members ready to take challenges.
